Final Project: Building Inventory Visualizations

Chart 1: Number of Buildings Constructed (5-Year Intervals)

This chart shows the number of university buildings constructed over time. The x-axis represents the year buildings were constructed, grouped into 5-year intervals. The y-axis shows how many buildings were built in each time bin. This helps identify periods of construction growth.

Chart 2: Gross Square Footage by Year (Interactive)

This interactive chart allows users to explore the total gross square footage by year of construction. A slider allows highlighting a specific year, helping to pinpoint spikes in building space expansion over time.
